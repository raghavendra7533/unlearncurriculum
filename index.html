WEEK 1

FIRST PRINCIPLES THINKING

Seeing What Others Miss

Week Objectives

  • • The paradigm shift: Product Managers coordinate teams. Product Makers build the future themselves.
  • • First principles thinking: Break problems down to their core when others stay on the surface
  • • Identify hidden assumptions that limit conventional thinkers
  • • Find root causes where others only see symptoms

Real Example: Food Delivery App

Traditional PMs: "Let's optimize driver routes to deliver food faster."

Product Makers: Using Palette AI to analyze the entire system, you discover the real bottleneck is restaurant capacity during peak hours. By implementing a predictive kitchen load balancing system, you reduce delivery times by 37% without changing a single driver route.

Detailed Assignments

  • Deconstruct a popular product to its first principles (written analysis of 1500+ words)
  • Identify 10 hidden assumptions in your current product thinking and create counter-arguments for each
  • Create a root cause analysis for a failed product using the 5 Whys methodology
  • Build a first principles map for your initial product concept, identifying at least 3 non-obvious insights
